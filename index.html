Privacy Policy - Ren-To-Hub

Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to Ren-To-Hub, a smart rental platform for video and photography equipment. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name
  • Phone number (OTP login)
  • Email address
  • User role (Customer / Vendor)
  • Location details

1.2 Equipment & Booking Data

  • Rental history
  • Selected equipment (camera, lens, drone, lighting, etc.)
  • Booking dates and duration
  • Payment and deposit status

1.3 Usage & Device Data

  • Device information
  • App activity and logs
  • Error reports and diagnostics

1.4 Location Data

  • Used to show nearby equipment vendors
  • Improve AI-based recommendations

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage rentals and bookings
  • To recommend equipment using AI
  • To connect users with nearby vendors
  • To improve app features and performance
  • To send booking updates and alerts

3. AI Usage

Ren-To-Hub uses AI to suggest suitable photography and video equipment based on your rental history, location, and usage behavior. No personal data is sold or misused.

4. Payments

Payments are processed securely via third-party payment gateways. We do not store debit/credit card details.

5. Data Security

  • Secure authentication
  • Encrypted data transfer (HTTPS)
  • Restricted access to sensitive data

6. User Rights

  • Access and update your data
  • Request data deletion
  • Delete your account

7. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (authentication & notifications)
  • Google Maps (location services)
  • Payment gateways

8. Children’s Privacy

Ren-To-Hub is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

9. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the app.
